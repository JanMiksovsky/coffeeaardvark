coffeeaardvark

Welcome to Coffeeaardvark, a site dedicated to the intersection of coffee and aardvarks! Learn more about the Coffeeaardvark and his family below. To bring Coffeeaardvark to your home, check out our online store.

Coffeeaardvark's grandparents emigrated from Africa to the United States, settling down to raise their aardvark family in Seattle, Washington. Coffeeaardvark is a sophmore at Aardvark University, where he is getting a degree in aardvark studies.

Coffeeaardvark lives near his family's home in Seattle. He has a younger sister, Peggy, who's currently in 11th grade. She says that she likes frappuccinos, but really she just likes ice cream. Peggy also likes to read books, as long as they are good books. 

Coffeeaardvark's dad is named George. George prefers a caffé breve. Coffeeaardvark also has a younger cousin named Jeremy. Whenever Jeremy visits, George likes to sit on his settee and share stories.

Jeremy is six years old, and will be turning seven in September. Jeremy gets hot chocolate instead of coffee (he thinks that coffee is just bitter water) but he will only drink the cocoa if the barista puts a fern leaf design on top.

Coffeeaardvark's mom is Maggie. She is on a parent committee at Peggy's school. The committee helps to organize and plan events. Maggie loves to eat toffee — she's a real toffeeaardvark.

Coffeeaardvark and his family wish you happy holidays this winter!
